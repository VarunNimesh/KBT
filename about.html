About our Hotel

Welcome to KBT, your perfect getaway in GERMANY. Since 1963, we have been dedicated to providing exceptional hospitality, offering comfortable accommodations designed for relaxation. Our rooms and suites come equipped with modern amenities, and our on-site restaurant serves delicious meals made from fresh, local ingredients.

At KBT, we pride ourselves on our attentive service, ensuring every guest feels at home. Discover the charm of GERMANY with our knowledgeable staff ready to assist you. Experience the perfect blend of comfort and hospitality at KBT, where your satisfaction is our top priority.
